BASIC MARKDOWN FORMAT
=====  
Headers
-----  
# # H1  
## ## H2  
### ### H3  
#### #### H4  
#####  ##### H5  
######  ###### H6  

Emphasis
-----
Italics with *asterisks* or _underscores_  
Strong emphasis (bold) with **double astrisks** or __double underscores__  
Combined emphasis with **asterisks and _underscores_**
